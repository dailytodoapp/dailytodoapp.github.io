Daily FAQ

How to share lists with Daily?

Click on a list and use the share button from the appbar. This will open a dialog that lets you send the link of your list. Keep in mind that the person you're sharing the list with must have Daily installed as well.

How does list sharing work?

No matter how many people you share your list with, changes will instantly appear across all devices simultaneously.

Does Daily work offline?

Yes! You don't need an internet connection to use Daily, lists and items can be added while you're offline without problems.

Can I change the language for voice input?

Of course! Open the main menu and go to the settings screen. You'll see an option to switch the language for voice input.

How to enable dark mode?

Check the settings page. You can set the app theme to light, dark, or system default.

How can I get rid of ads?

Daily lets you make a one-time payment to remove ads forever! Check the "remove ads" option in the main menu.

I purchased premium but still see ads. Why?

It might take a bit for Google to confirm your purchase. Restart the app and everything should be fine.
